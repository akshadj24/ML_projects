About the Project

This Machine Learning project is designed to help educators and students understand the factors that influence academic success. By analyzing demographic data, parental education levels, and preparation courses, our AI model predicts the likely math scores of students.

Built with Python, Flask, and scikit-learn, this end-to-end web application demonstrates the power of data science in predicting educational outcomes and providing actionable insights for student development.
